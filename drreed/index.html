The Dr. Reed

Earlier this year, the Ottawa Tavern introduced the Dr. Reed, a pitcher of ice, filled with PBR and a handful of limes. It was introduced to us by Dustin Hostetler, who brought it from Chicago where it was invented by Harper Reed. Since then, we’ve expanded our selection to include 6 “Reed” pitchers.

DR. REED
Our original PBR pitcher drink. PBR over ice served with a handful of limes. $4.00

SENOR REED
Half PBR, half margarita in a pitcher. Served over ice with a handful of limes. $10.00

REED MY LIPS
Half a pitcher of PBR topped off with rum, vodka, midori, cranberry juice, a splash of grenadine and sour mix. Served over ice. $12.00

ELECTRIC REED
Half a pitcher of PBR topped off with lemon rum, raspberry vodka, blue curacao, and a splash of soda. Served with a handful of lemons. Served over ice. $12.00

OLD MAN McREED
A bottle of cider, a can of Guinness, and two shots of whiskey. Served in a pitcher over ice.

LONG ISLAND ICED REED
Half PBR, half Long Island Iced Tea in a pitcher. Served over ice with a handful of lemons. $12.00
